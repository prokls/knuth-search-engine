Frequently Asked Questions

Who runs this search engine?

See impressum.

What document are supposed to be uploaded?

Any document with educational background. As far as the idea for this project came to my mind during my studies, documents are intended to be part of courses at TU Graz.

Why is this website written in english? Which language should the documents be written in?

Distribution of educational documents is more than just "sharing with your friends". That's the reason why your documents should be for a wide range of students and to reach a large target audience, you should select the appropriate language. English might be a good idea for most document, but if you are not familiar with the language, you would like to use, prefer your mother tongue.

What data are you storing about me?

None, beside those, you enter explicitly when uploading.

What fonts did you use in the logo?

Computer modern by Donald E. Knuth.

Why is it called knuth?

Sorry. As a CS student, I'm a big fan of Donald E. Knuth.

Which license does the source code have?

The whole website and search engine is licensed under GPLv3. I prefer the copyleft clause for this project.

Where did you stole the filetype icons from?

I love the work of the GNOME designers. Some filetype icons are taken from "Dropline! Neu" theme by silvestre herrea under the Gnu GPLv2.

Where did you stole the syntax from?

Well, actually I have designed my own syntax before. I was aware of the need of "terms", "intervalls" and "key-value-pairs". Those were my initial implementation targets. You will find those elements as "term", "range" and "field" in Syntax again. However... I stole the symbols (:, ") from search engines like Google, Yahoo and wolframalpha, because I wanted to keep as user-friendly as possible.
